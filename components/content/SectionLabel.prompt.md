**SectionLabel** — the brand's signature section header: a lowercase mono word, optionally numbered, above a block of content. This is lifted straight from the source site's `education / experience / projects` labels.

```jsx
<SectionLabel index="01" align="left">projects</SectionLabel>
<SectionLabel>experience</SectionLabel>
```

Always lowercase. Center-aligned in the classic slim-column layout; left-aligned in wider two-column layouts. The `index` gives a subtle numbered rhythm to a stack of sections.
